Explanation

The purpose of this page is to randomly generate characters for the Into the Odd RPG by Chris McDowall. You will be shown an image of the sheet and a link for downloading an 8.5x11 PDF of it. If you don't like the rolls, feel free to use the back button to come back to this page and try again.

You can get one, two, or four characters on a sheet, by choosing one of the options above.
