The purpose of this page is to randomly generate characters for the Into the Odd RPG by Chris McDowall. You will be presented with a printable 11x8.5 pdf that you can save a print if you like the character/s on it. If you don't like it/them, feel free to use the back button to come back to this page and try again.

You can get one, two, or four characters on a sheet, by choosing one of the options above.
